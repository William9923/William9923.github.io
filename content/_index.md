# William Ong

Software Engineer at [Grab](https://www.grab.com/), working on [GrabInsure](https://www.grabinsure.sg/) backend systems in Go, Python, and Java.

I've been building software since 2020 and working professionally since 2022. Previously, I worked at [Gojek](https://www.gojek.io/) on the Fulfillment (Allocation) team and at [Shopee](https://shopee.co.id) on the Payment team.

I care about building systems that scale, stay maintainable, and remain simple enough to extend as the future demands.

> Keep it simple, and don't build what you don't need yet.

Outside of work, I read open source codebases, tinker with my [dotfiles](https://github.com/William9923/dotfiles), contribute (a bit) to the tools I use, exercise, and collect Pokemon cards.
